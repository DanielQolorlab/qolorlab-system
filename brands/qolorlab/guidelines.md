# QOLORLAB — Brand Guidelines (v0)

## Core Principle
Structured systems over improvisation.

## Visual Tone
- Dark base (near-black / deep navy)
- Minimal surfaces
- High contrast hierarchy
- No noise, no decoration

## Accent
- Primary: Red (#FD0611)
- Use sparingly for:
  - signals
  - active states
  - emphasis

## Typography
- Clean, neutral sans-serif
- Strong hierarchy
- No playful styling

## Spacing
- Systematic spacing (8px base)
- Generous breathing room
- No cramped layouts

## Motion
- Spring-based
- Smooth, no bounce
- Controlled, intentional
- Never decorative

## Composition Rules
- Centered or clearly structured layouts
- Max width constraints
- Always readable first, aesthetic second

## Philosophy
Clarity > Expression  
Structure > Chaos  
Systems > Prompts
